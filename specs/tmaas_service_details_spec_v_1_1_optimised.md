# Specification: Service Details Page (Stage 01) — DQ TMaaS Platform

---

## Story: The Structured Transformation Product Page

As a prospective client or enterprise buyer, I want to view a comprehensive, transparent breakdown of a specific transformation service, so that I understand exactly what deliverables I am purchasing, the expected business outcomes, the delivery methodology, and the fixed commercial constraints before committing to an engagement.

### The Product Page Backbone

To shift away from traditional consulting proposals (SOWs) generated after lengthy discovery cycles, this page acts as the definitive source of truth for a TMaaS transformation offering:

1. **Commercial Clarity (The Hero Layer)**
   - Eliminates ambiguity immediately by placing the service type, timeline, pricing, category, and delivery structure at the very top of the page.
   - Reinforces that TMaaS services follow standardized delivery models rather than open-ended consulting engagements.

2. **Transformation Structure (The Methodology Layer)**
   - Maps the service into a structured transformation journey.
   - Explains the step-by-step delivery model so buyers understand exactly how the service is executed under TMaaS governance.
   - Methodology varies depending on whether the offering is a Design service, Deploy service, or Bundle.

3. **Trust & Activation (The Commerce Layer)**
   - A sticky activation panel ensures the primary actions remain visible at all times.
   - AI Guidance supports users who are unsure whether the service matches their transformation needs.

4. **Progressive Discovery (The Ecosystem Layer)**
   - Encourages users to discover related services, bundles, and recommended next transformation steps.
   - Reinforces that services operate as part of broader transformation pathways rather than isolated consulting activities.

---

## Purpose (Why):

The Service Details Page serves as the Product Details Page (PDP) for TMaaS.

- **Reduce Sales Friction**: Make the service scope, outputs, timelines, and methodology completely transparent.
- **Reinforce Productization**: Position services as structured transformation products with standardized delivery models.
- **Set Governance Expectations**: Reinforce that delivery follows architecture-led governance and milestone-based execution.
- **Guide Transformation Discovery**: Help users understand adjacent services, bundles, and transformation pathways.
- **Drive Activation**: Enable users to confidently start services, add them to cart, or consult AI Guidance.

---

# 6. Navigation & UX Principles

- **Sticky Commerce**: The activation panel remains visible throughout the browsing experience.
- **Two-Column Layout**: Separates transformation reading flow from transactional activation.
- **Structured Productization**: Services should feel like standardized transformation products rather than generic consulting engagements.
- **Variant-Based Rendering**: Design, Deploy, and Bundle pages render different methodology and output structures while maintaining a unified shell.
- **AI-Guided Discovery**: AI Advisor helps users navigate uncertainty and identify appropriate transformation pathways.
- **Transformation Ecosystem Positioning**: The page should reinforce that services exist within broader transformation journeys.
- **Graceful Error Handling**: Invalid service IDs should render a clean "Service Not Found" experience with recovery actions.

---

- Custom quotation generators

# 8. Expected Outcomes

- **Pre-Qualified Demand**: Users entering checkout already understand scope, outputs, timeline, and delivery methodology.
- **Reduced Sales Friction**: Transparent structure significantly reduces pre-sales clarification cycles.
- **Standardized Expectations**: Clients clearly understand how TMaaS delivery operates.
- **Bundle Discovery**: Increased adoption of grouped transformation pathways.
- **Transformation Progression**: Users progressively discover broader transformation opportunities.
- **AI-Assisted Activation**: AI Advisor reduces uncertainty for users unsure how to proceed.
- **Stronger Productization**: Services feel structured, scalable, and commercially transparent.
